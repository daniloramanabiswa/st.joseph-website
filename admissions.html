Admissions Process

Upon successful completion of your training, you will receive a recognized Certificate of Competency to advance your career and skills.

How to Apply

  1. Check Eligibility: Applicants must be aged 16+ and committed to vocational training.
  2. Choose a Program: Explore our Programs and select the one that fits your passion.
  3. Complete Application: Fill out the application form available at our center or download it here.
  4. Submit Documents: Provide identification, proof of residence, and educational certificates.
  5. Attend Interview: Selected candidates will be invited for an interview to discuss goals and readiness.
  6. Enrollment Confirmation: Successful applicants will receive an enrollment letter and schedule.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 16 years
  • Basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Commitment to complete the full course duration
  • Willingness to participate in practical training

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer scholarships based on merit and need. Our goal is to ensure no one is left behind due to financial hardship.

Contact our admissions office for more details or to apply.
