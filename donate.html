Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?
The easiest way is using the donation form above. You can also donate via bank transfer or contact us for other methods.
Can I dedicate my donation to a specific project?
Yes! You can specify the donation purpose in the form or in your transfer details.
Why are regular donations helpful?
Regular donations help us plan better and sustain long-term programs that empower youth in Karamoja.
Is my donation secure?
Yes, we use secure payment processing and never share your data with third parties.
How much of my donation goes to the projects?
Over 90% of donations directly support programs, with the rest covering administration and outreach.
