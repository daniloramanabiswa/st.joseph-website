Catering & Food Service

Develop professional culinary skills for the hospitality industry

6 Months Certificate Max 12 students

Program Overview

Our Catering & Food Service program trains students in professional kitchen skills, food safety, and business management for careers in restaurants, hotels, and catering businesses.

What You'll Learn

  • Food preparation and cooking techniques
  • Menu planning and costing
  • Food safety and hygiene
  • Baking and pastry fundamentals
  • Customer service skills
  • Small business management

Course Structure

Module 1: Culinary Basics

Knife skills, cooking methods, and kitchen safety

Module 2: Baking Fundamentals

Bread, pastry, and dessert preparation

Module 3: Food Business

Menu planning, costing, and small business management
