Automotive Mechanics

Master vehicle maintenance and repair with hands-on training

6 Months Certificate Max 15 students

Program Overview

Our Automotive Mechanics program provides comprehensive training in vehicle maintenance, engine repair, and diagnostics using modern equipment. Students gain practical skills for employment in garages, dealerships, or starting their own repair businesses.

What You'll Learn

  • Engine systems and components
  • Electrical systems and diagnostics
  • Brake and suspension systems
  • Transmission and drivetrain
  • Preventive maintenance
  • Workshop safety and management

Course Structure

Module 1: Fundamentals

Introduction to automotive systems, tools, and safety procedures

Module 2: Engine Systems

Engine components, operation, and troubleshooting

Module 3: Electrical Systems

Wiring, batteries, alternators, and electronic components
