Our Mission

St. Joseph the Worker Training Centre provides vocational training to underserved youth in Karamoja, Uganda. We strive to equip youth with the necessary vocational skills that empower economic growth and independence.

  • Automotive Mechanics
  • Catering & Hospitality
  • Carpentry & Construction
  • Brick Laying & Concrete Practice
  • Sustainable Agriculture
  • Tailoring

Guided by our motto: "Enter to Learn, Go to Serve."
