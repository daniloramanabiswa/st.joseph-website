Our Mission

St. Joseph the Worker Training Centre, a project of Kayla Peace Village, provides free vocational training to underserved youth in Kosike, Karamoja. We combat poverty through education in mechanics, catering, agriculture, and more, guided by our motto: "Enter to Learn, Go to Serve."
